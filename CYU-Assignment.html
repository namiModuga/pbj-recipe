Simple And Easy Omelette Recipe

Learn how to make a simple and easy omelette.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (Your choice of amount)
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Pepper Powder And Chilli Powder (Optional)

Other Items Needed

  • A pan based on how many eggs being used. (1-2 = Small, 3-6 = Medium, 6+ = Large)
  • A spoon
  • A plate
  • Stove
  • A Bowl

Procedure (For 1 Egg Omelette)

  1. Place the pan on the stove and turn on the stove to low heat and heat up the pan.
  2. While the pan is heating up crack your egg using a spoon into the bowl.
  3. Add salt based on your taste and add pepper pepper and chilli powder if you are adding some. (Remember chilli powder and pepper powder are optional but will enhance your flavours if you add them)
  4. Once you have added the spice and optional spices, beat the egg until it is a water consistency.
  5. Once you feel heat coming from the pan, add a tablespoon of oil on the pan.
  6. Add your beaten egg into the pan and make sure the egg covers the whole pan.
  7. Increase the heat to medium and let it cook for about 3-4 mins.
  8. After about 3-4 mins use your spoon to flip the omelette and cook the other side for another 3-4 mins.
  9. After about 3-4 mins, flip the omelette again and see if both sides are golden yellow. (Some white parts should also be present)(Check image above for visual representation)
  10. Lift omelette off the pan with spoon and place on plate.
  11. Garnish with anything of your choice.
  12. Enjoy!
